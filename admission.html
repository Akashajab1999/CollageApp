How to Apply

Obtain the admission form from the college office or download it online. Fill out all required details and submit along with:

  • Academic mark sheets
  • Identity proof
  • Passport-sized photograph

Selection Process

Admissions are based on academic performance and seat availability. Admission is open once a year, usually before the new academic session.

Important Notes

  • Contact the college office for exact dates and fees.
  • Early submission is recommended due to limited seats.
