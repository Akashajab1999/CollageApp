About Gramonnati Mandal’s College

Tatyasaheb Bhujbal Agri Polytechnic & Dairy Institute (LMDP) envisions empowering rural youth and advancing education in the agriculture and dairy sectors. The college provides a blend of practical, hands-on training, modern technology, and research-based learning to prepare students for careers in livestock management and dairy production.

Our institute is strategically located at Tatyasaheb Bhujbal Krishi Shikshan Sankul on the Old Pune–Nashik Highway, Narayangaon, Pune, providing students with a rural learning environment while maintaining connectivity to urban centers.

History & Background

The college is a part of Gramonnati Mandal, a renowned organization dedicated to rural development and education. Since its inception, the institute has aimed to bridge the gap between traditional agriculture and modern scientific practices. Over the years, it has established itself as a center of excellence in livestock management, dairy technology, and allied agricultural studies.

Objectives

  • To provide high-quality technical education in agriculture and dairy sciences.
  • To develop professional skills and entrepreneurial capabilities among students.
  • To encourage sustainable farming and livestock management practices.
  • To foster research, innovation, and community outreach in rural areas.
  • To prepare graduates capable of contributing to regional and national agricultural development.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a leading institute in livestock management and dairy production, empowering students for sustainable growth in agriculture and rural development.

Mission

To provide skill-based education, hands-on practical training, research opportunities, and entrepreneurship guidance, preparing students for professional excellence in livestock and dairy industries.

Facilities & Infrastructure

  • Modern dairy training labs and research facilities.
  • Livestock farms and demonstration plots for practical learning.
  • Computer labs, classrooms, and library with updated resources.
  • Hostel facilities for students from distant regions.
  • Workshops, seminars, and industry visits to enhance practical exposure.

Achievements

  • Graduated over 500 skilled professionals in livestock and dairy management.
  • Active collaboration with Maharashtra Animal & Fishery Sciences University (MAFSU), Nagpur.
  • Recognized for excellence in rural entrepreneurship training.
  • Continuous contributions to research, training, and community development programs.

Why Choose Us?

  • Focus on practical and hands-on training with modern technology.
  • Experienced faculty with industry and research expertise.
  • Strong emphasis on entrepreneurship and rural development.
  • Well-equipped infrastructure for academic and professional growth.
  • Opportunities for research, fieldwork, and skill enhancement programs.
