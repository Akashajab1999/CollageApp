Institute Overview

The College of Livestock Management & Dairy Production is managed by Gramonnati Mandal, Narayangaon. The institute was established to uplift rural society through quality education and practical training in livestock and dairy sectors.

Vision

To develop skilled professionals in livestock management and dairy production and promote self-employment in rural areas.

Mission

  • Provide hands-on practical training
  • Introduce modern dairy and animal husbandry techniques
  • Encourage entrepreneurship among rural youth
  • Support sustainable dairy development
