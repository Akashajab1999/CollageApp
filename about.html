College Overview

The College of Livestock Management and Dairy Production is a specialized institute that trains students in modern livestock management and dairy production techniques. It is affiliated with Maharashtra Animal & Fishery Sciences University, Nagpur.

Objectives

  • Provide technical and practical education in livestock management
  • Promote rural employment through dairy and animal husbandry skills
  • Introduce modern technology in the dairy sector
  • Develop entrepreneurship skills for small-scale dairy businesses

Facilities

  • Practical dairy labs
  • Animal husbandry training farm
  • Experienced faculty with industry knowledge
  • Library and study resources
