About Gramonnati Mandal’s College

Gramonnati Mandal’s College of Livestock Management & Dairy Production provides practical and skill-based education to train students in livestock management and dairy production. Our programs focus on modern technologies, hands-on training, and rural entrepreneurship.

Principal’s Message

Welcome to our esteemed college. We aim to combine modern education with practical experience to prepare students for future challenges in livestock and dairy industries.

Chairman’s Message

Our college stands for excellence in education and research. We provide students with the best opportunities to learn, innovate, and succeed in the dairy and livestock sector.

Vision

To be a leading institute in livestock management and dairy production, empowering students for sustainable growth in agriculture.

Mission

To provide skill-based education, practical training, and research opportunities preparing students for professional excellence.

Achievements

  • Established modern dairy training labs and research facilities.
  • Graduated over 500 skilled professionals in livestock and dairy management.
  • Active collaborations with Maharashtra Animal & Fishery Sciences University.
  • Recognized for excellence in rural entrepreneurship training.
