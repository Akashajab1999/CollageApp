Institute Overview

The College of Livestock Management & Dairy Production is managed by Gramonnati Mandal, Narayangaon. The institute was established to uplift rural society through quality education and practical training.

Vision

To develop skilled professionals in livestock and dairy sectors and promote self-employment in rural areas.

Mission

To provide hands-on training, modern dairy knowledge, and entrepreneurial guidance to students.
