Diploma in Livestock Management and Dairy Production

Duration: 2 Years

Course Details

  • Animal husbandry techniques
  • Dairy farming and milk production
  • Milk processing, storage, and quality control
  • Rural entrepreneurship and farm management

Eligibility

Students must have completed 10th grade (SSC) or equivalent.

Career Opportunities

  • Dairy Farm Manager
  • Livestock Supervisor
  • Small-scale Dairy Entrepreneur
  • Quality Control Technician in Dairy Industry
