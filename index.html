About Institute

The Gramonnati Mandal Institute of Narayangaon was founded by the late Guruvarya R. P. Sabnis with the vision of uplifting rural society through education. The institute has been a pioneer in agricultural and allied education in northern Pune district.

The institute strongly believes in combining education with rural life. Through the Diploma in Livestock Management & Dairy Production, students receive hands-on training, industry exposure, and entrepreneurial guidance.

Chairman’s Message

Our aim is to empower rural youth through quality education and skill development. We strive to create responsible professionals for the livestock and dairy sector.

Principal’s Message

We focus on practical learning, discipline, and innovation to prepare students for real-world challenges in livestock and dairy production.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a leading institute in livestock education.

Mission: Skill-based education, research, and rural development.
