Welcome

Welcome to the College of Livestock Management and Dairy Production, Narayangaon. Run by Gramonnati Mandal, this college is affiliated with Maharashtra Animal & Fishery Sciences University, Nagpur.

We offer practical training in animal husbandry, dairy business, milk processing, and rural entrepreneurship. Our goal is to empower rural youth with employable skills and modern technology knowledge.
