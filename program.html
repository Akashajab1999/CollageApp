Programme Offered

The college offers a Diploma in Livestock Management & Dairy Production, under the affiliation of Maharashtra Animal & Fishery Sciences University (MAFSU), Nagpur. The course is designed to provide skill-based education for students aiming to work in livestock and dairy sectors.

Course Name Eligibility Affiliation
Diploma in Livestock Management & Dairy Production 10+2 / Equivalent (Science / Agriculture Stream) MAFSU, Nagpur

Course Highlights:

  • Hands-on training in livestock management and dairy operations
  • Skill development for rural entrepreneurship
  • Exposure to modern dairy technologies and practices
  • Preparation for employment in livestock and dairy sectors
