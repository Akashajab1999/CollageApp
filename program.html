Programme Offered

The institute offers various diploma and certificate level programmes aimed at skill development, entrepreneurship, and employment generation in agriculture, livestock, dairy, and allied sectors.

Course Name Eligibility Affiliation / Nature
Diploma in Livestock Management & Dairy Production 10+2 / Equivalent (Science / Agriculture Stream) MAFSU, Nagpur
Agriculture Polytechnic 10th / 10+2 (As per norms) Recognized Polytechnic Programme
Mali Training Certificate Course 8th / 10th Pass Skill-based Certificate Course
Diploma in Agro Journalism 10+2 / Graduate (Preferred) Agricultural Communication Programme
Diploma in Agri-Business Management 10+2 / Graduate Agri-Business & Management Studies
Diploma in Horticulture 10th / 10+2 Horticulture & Crop Production

Course Highlights:

  • Practical-oriented and skill-based education
  • Focus on agriculture, livestock, dairy, and allied sectors
  • Entrepreneurship and self-employment opportunities
  • Training aligned with rural and agribusiness needs
  • Career-oriented programmes with industry relevance
